Applications of XAI

Explainable AI is applied across various industries to enhance transparency and trust in AI-driven decisions.

Key Industries Utilizing XAI

  • Healthcare: AI-assisted diagnostics with interpretable predictions.
  • Finance: Fraud detection models with transparent decision-making.
  • Legal and Law Enforcement: AI-based risk assessments ensuring fairness.
  • Autonomous Vehicles: Explainable driving decisions for safety compliance.
  • E-commerce: AI recommendation systems with user-understandable explanations.
