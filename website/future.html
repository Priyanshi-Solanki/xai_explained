Future of XAI

Explainable AI is continuously evolving with cutting-edge research and innovation shaping its trajectory.

Next Evolution in XAI

  • Neuro-symbolic AI: Combining neural networks and symbolic reasoning for better interpretability.
  • Interactive XAI: AI that allows real-time user interaction for deeper understanding.
  • Regulation-driven XAI: Growing global policies demanding AI transparency.

Timeline of XAI Evolution

From early expert systems to modern deep learning explainability methods:

  • 1980s - Early Expert Systems
  • 1990s - Rule-based AI and Transparent Decision Models
  • 2000s - Rise of Black-box AI
  • 2010s - Post-hoc Explainability Methods Emerge
  • 2020s - XAI Integration into AI Regulations
